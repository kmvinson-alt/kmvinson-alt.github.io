Scrambled Egg
eggs are one of my favorite foods, here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled egg.
Ingredients Methods
  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream
    		•
    1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
    2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
    3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
    4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you a making an omelette)
    5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
